Names: 5 status glyphs (status-not-started/applied/under-review/needs-changes/approved), domain (document, upload, passport, diploma, calendar, chat, phone, email, payment), top-bar (search, bell, settings, chevron-down, logout). Status icons must take their status colour, never neutral.
